Curious about why enterprise email security is undergoing a massive upgrade?

**The Problem:** Standard email can be intercepted, read, or entirely forged by hackers completely undetected. Even worse, if a future quantum computer breaks today's email encryption, it instantly exposes decades of successfully archived sensitive messages.
**The Solution:** Organizations must upgrade their entire email infrastructure to use quantum-proof, mathematically unforgeable digital signatures and encryption wrappers.
**The Strategy:** Because email requires both the sender and receiver to understand the encryption, IT teams must issue "dual-certificates" so both modern and older email systems remain secure during the multi-year transition.
**The Ecosystem:** Overhauling global email security relies heavily on major email providers, corporate IT departments, and enterprise security gateways.
