### What This Is About

Your organization's quantum timeline is limited by your slowest vendor. Third-party software, hardware, and cloud providers handling sensitive data are massive, immediate targets for "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" campaigns.

### Why It Matters

A single vendor dependency relying purely on classical encryption can completely block your organization's compliance with upcoming mandates (like NSA CNSA 2.0 or FIPS 140-3) and create a permanent security blind spot.

### The Key Takeaway

Organizations must mandate the delivery of a Crypto Bill of Materials (CBOM), such as CycloneDX 1.6+, from all vendors. This standardized inventory reveals all algorithms, allowing automatic scoring of vendor readiness and enabling strict contractual deadlines.
