A Virtual Private Network (VPN) creates a secure, encrypted tunnel directly through the public internet so you can safely access your corporate network from places like a public coffee shop Wi-Fi.

Because the math locking that tunnel today is totally vulnerable to quantum computers, hackers could record your traffic right now and decrypt your company secrets tomorrow. Upgrading to a "Quantum-Safe VPN" radically reinforces that tunnel with new, impenetrable Post-Quantum math, slamming the door on any "harvest now, decrypt later" spying efforts.
