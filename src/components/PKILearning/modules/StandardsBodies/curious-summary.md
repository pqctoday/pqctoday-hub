### What This Is About

The post-quantum ecosystem involves three distinct roles: Standards bodies (like NIST) write the algorithm specifications, Certification bodies (like CMVP) test the products, and Compliance frameworks (like CNSA 2.0) mandate their use in specific sectors.

### Why It Matters

Confusing these roles is a common source of compliance failure. For example, purchasing a product that merely uses a "NIST algorithm" is insufficient if your framework requires a CMVP FIPS 140-3 validation, which itself has an 18-36 month certification backlog.

### The Key Takeaway

You comply with a framework, get certified against a standard, and the standard is written by the standard body. Multinational teams must track NIST globally, ETSI in the EU, and specific national mandates (like BSI in Germany or OSCCA in China).
