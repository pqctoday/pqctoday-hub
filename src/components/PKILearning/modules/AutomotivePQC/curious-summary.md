Curious about how smart cars defend against hackers?

**The Problem:** Modern cars are complex rolling computers that constantly broadcast safety messages to other vehicles. A future quantum computer could forge these messages and cause real-world digital accidents (like phantom braking).
**The Solution:** Automotive manufacturers must upgrade car computer systems to use advanced, quantum-proof digital signatures that absolutely cannot be forged.
**The Strategy:** Because cars have dozens of tiny separate computers inside them, designers are consolidating them into a few powerful, centralized "zone controllers" that have the processing power to handle this advanced security in real-time.
**The Ecosystem:** This total architectural shift impacts car manufacturers, traffic infrastructure planners, automotive regulators, and microchip designers.
