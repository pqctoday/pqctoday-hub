Curious about how to protect tiny, ancient computers?

**The Problem:** Smart sensors and critical industrial control systems often have significantly less memory than a 1990s pocket calculator. The massive new quantum-proof encryption locks being standardized simply cannot fit inside these incredibly constrained, low-power physical devices.
**The Solution:** The industry must deploy highly optimized, ultra-lightweight cryptographic algorithms specifically engineered for minimal hardware and tiny battery budgets.
**The Strategy:** Where mathematical upgrades are physically impossible, industrial operators must use heavy-duty "edge gateways" to translate standard encryption into quantum-proof security on behalf of the vulnerable devices.
**The Ecosystem:** Protecting critical infrastructure components relies on hardware manufacturers, industrial grid operators, and IoT platform designers.
