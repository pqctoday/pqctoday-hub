### What This Is About

Post-Quantum Cryptography is not a "solved" problem. The global transition requires researchers and academics to actively test, improve, and break these new algorithms. We need minds dedicated to lattice-based cryptanalysis, side-channel attack mitigation, and formal verification.

### Why It Matters

Standardized algorithms (like ML-KEM and ML-DSA) are just the beginning. Without rigorous academic peer-review and continuous cryptanalysis, undiscovered vulnerabilities could compromise future global security infrastructures before they are even fully deployed.

### The Key Takeaway

We need researchers to improve post-quantum cryptography. Experiment with NIST algorithms, hunt for side-channel physical vulnerabilities, collaborate across disciplines, and secure grant funding by advancing the frontier of quantum-safe security.
