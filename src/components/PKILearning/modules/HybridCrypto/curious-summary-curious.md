Upgrading the world's computers to use entirely new Post-Quantum math isn't like flipping a simple light switch; it takes years of meticulous testing and auditing.

To remain perfectly safe during this chaotic transition, security architects are using "Hybrid Cryptography". This means they lock their sensitive data using both the tried-and-true old math AND the brand new Post-Quantum math simultaneously. If either method turns out to have a hidden, fatal flaw, the data remains fully protected by the other lock, providing a foolproof safety net for critical enterprise systems.
