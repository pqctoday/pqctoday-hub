### What This Is About

PQC risk management provides a framework to prepare for the arrival of a Cryptographically Relevant Quantum Computer (CRQC). It involves modeling CRQC timelines to understand when threats become real and identifying which cryptographic assets (like RSA and ECC) are exposed.

### Why It Matters

A CRQC will break the asymmetric cryptography that protects virtually all digital communications today. Relying on Risk Quantification (evaluating Likelihood x Impact) is essential to prioritize migration efforts, especially because of Harvest-Now-Decrypt-Later (HNDL) attacks targeting long-lived secrets.

### The Key Takeaway

To align with stringent compliance deadlines like CNSA 2.0, organizations must adopt a three-step process: CRQC scenario planning, building a comprehensive risk register, and visualizing exposure on a 5x5 likelihood-impact heatmap to guide executive decision-making.
