## FISMA-NIST-SP-800-53r5

- **Source**: NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 — Security and Privacy Controls (FISMA baseline)
- **URL**: https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/SpecialPublications/NIST.SP.800-53r5.pdf
- **Requirement count**: 6
- **Assurance / FIPS**:
  - _T2 Risk-Informed · all_: Assess the effectiveness of implemented security and privacy controls to ensure trustworthiness.
  - _T2 Risk-Informed · all_: Continuously monitor information systems to maintain confidence in security and privacy capabilities.
  - _T2 Risk-Informed · all_: Provide assurance regarding the strength of functions and mechanisms provided by security controls.
- **Governance**:
  - _T2 Risk-Informed · all_: Establish a comprehensive risk management program to categorize systems and select security controls meeting mission needs.
  - _T2 Risk-Informed · all_: Develop and maintain security and privacy controls as part of an organization-wide process to manage risk.
  - _T2 Risk-Informed · all_: Ensure leaders at all levels are accountable for ensuring readiness and security of information systems.
